***less adored bio but necessary for now:***

Kathleen Ma is an artist and writer based in Queens, NY. Her work often manifests as 3d renderings, videos, photos, writings, and zines. She has exhibited work at the Center for Book Arts, NYC Tech Zine Fair, LA Tiny Tech Zines, Boston Art Book Fair, Odds & Ends Art Book Fair, and Chinatown Zine Soup. Kathleen was previously a 2019 Workplace Resident at the Center for Book Arts and a member of the independent publishing initiative printjob.

Kathleen graduated from Princeton University with degrees in visual art + public policy. Some of her past and present day work has been with the Climate Jobs National Resource Center, Strategic Organizing Center, NYC Department of Sanitation, Partner & Partners, and the San Francisco Planning Department.
